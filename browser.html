Data Browser

The TIDE has completed a variety of large-scale in vivo screens to discover novel mechanisms of resistance and enhanced sensitivity to cancer immunotherapy.

Screens have been performed in vitro, in immunodeficient mice (NSG), immunocompetent wild-type mice (WT), and wild-type mice treated with immune checkpoint blockade therapy (ICB).

We have created an interactive browser to explore the results of these screens at tumorimmunity.org.
